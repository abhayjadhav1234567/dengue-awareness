What is Dengue?

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection causing flu-like illness. Severe dengue can be life-threatening if not treated in time.

Prevention Tips

  • Remove stagnant water around homes.
  • Use mosquito nets while sleeping.
  • Wear full-sleeve clothes.
  • Keep surroundings clean and dry.
  • Use mosquito repellents regularly.

Treatment Steps

Treatment Step What It Means Why It Is Important
Rest & Hydration Bed rest, drink water, ORS, juices, soups Prevents dehydration from fever and plasma leakage
Fever Control Use Paracetamol only (avoid aspirin/ibuprofen) Controls fever safely without increasing bleeding
Nutrition Light, easy-to-digest food (fruits, porridge, soups) Supports recovery and boosts immunity
Monitoring Regular health check-ups & symptom monitoring Ensures timely intervention if condition worsens
