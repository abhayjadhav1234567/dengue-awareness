What is Dengue?

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection caused by the bite of the Aedes aegypti mosquito. Severe dengue can be life-threatening.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Muscle & joint pains
  • Nausea & vomiting
